# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to a respectful and inclusive project environment. Contributors
should be able to participate without harassment, intimidation, or personal attacks.

## Expected Behavior

- Use welcoming and respectful language.
- Accept constructive feedback.
- Focus disagreement on technical ideas and project outcomes.
- Respect differing experience levels, backgrounds, and viewpoints.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or threats.
- Sexualized language or imagery.
- Trolling, insults, or sustained disruption.
- Publishing private information without permission.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close threads, reject contributions, temporarily limit
participation, or permanently ban participants who violate this code.

## Scope

This code applies in project repositories, issue trackers, pull requests, community spaces, and
other public project interactions.

## Attribution

This policy follows the intent of Contributor Covenant 2.1 while keeping project-specific wording.
